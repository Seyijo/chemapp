SEPERATION TECHNIQUES

One of the major activities in chemistry is the analysis of materials found around us. Many materials around us are mixtures, and a need to seperate the consituents of these mixtures is a major concern to chemists. In chemistry, the seperation of constituents of mixtures involves the use of simple principles and application of special eqiupments.
There are different type of mixtures:
  1. Mixture of solid and liquid e.g precipitates.
  2. Mixture of liquid and gas e.g carbonated drinks.
  3. Mixture of solid and solid e.g alloys like bronze.
  4. Mixture of gases e.g air.
The constituents of mixtures are seperated using their physical properties such as boiling point,melting point,solubility,density e.t.c.
